Stephen Hawking

1942-2018

Biography

Stephen Hawking (1942-2018) was a renowned theoretical physicist known for his groundbreaking work on black holes and cosmology. Diagnosed with motor neuron disease at 21, he defied all odds and continued his research. His bestseller "A Brief History of Time" made complex science accessible to the public. Hawking's legacy inspires scientists and the general public worldwide.
